## 12. FAQ

- **eyebrow:** FAQ
- **title:** Frequent questions

- **Q1:** How deeply do you learn our industry before the visit?
- **A1:** Enough to speak your language. I study the context, core processes, and tools you've already tried. The goal of prep isn't to become an industry expert — it's to quickly see where AI genuinely matters for you specifically.

- **Q2:** What do we actually walk away with?
- **A2:** A structured document: priorities, AI applicability map, complexity assessment, and a next-step roadmap. Plus — a shared language your team keeps using a week later.

- **Q3:** Do you implement AI solutions or only consult?
- **A3:** This is an advisory format. I help you decide what, why, and in what order. Implementation is done by your teams or integrators — and you'll be able to work with them intelligently after the session.

- **Q4:** Why not one intense 8-hour day?
- **A4:** Executives rarely perform well for 6–8 hours straight. Dense 2-hour sessions over several days with pauses for reflection produce radically better results than a marathon.

- **Q5:** Do you work remotely?
- **A5:** The main format is on-site. I take remote as an exception — for follow-up sessions. Being in the room with the team produces a qualitatively different result.

- **Q6:** How do I know which package fits?
- **A6:** Fill out the short form — I'll be honest. If Core fits, I won't sell you Executive. If the scope is wider, I'll say so.
